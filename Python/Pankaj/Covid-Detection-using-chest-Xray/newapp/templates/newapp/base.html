AI-powered Covid-19 Detection using X-ray images



With the mission to defeat coronavirus, we the students of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology had developed this website for the rapid testing of Covid-19 cases. Ensuring high accuracy results using less amount of time. This website is completely free to use to support our covid warriors in this pandemic.

The AI models are tested on thousands of images to ensure a high probability of giving accurate result. Our product identifies whether the patient has Covid, Pneumonia, Normal or any other Lung Infection. The only requirement to use this website is that you must have a photocopy of the recently taken chest X-Ray image, both posteroanterior and anteroposterior type of images will work for the models.
